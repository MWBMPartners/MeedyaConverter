MeedyaConverter is the all-in-one, easy-to-use multimedia converter for macOS. Convert audio, video, subtitles, and optical discs between hundreds of formats with professional-grade quality and a clean, native interface.

KEY FEATURES

• Hybrid encoding engine — FFmpeg subprocess for direct builds, FFmpegKit for App Store builds
• Video: H.264, H.265/HEVC, VP9, AV1, VVC, ProRes (422 & 4444), DNxHR, CFHD, NotchLC, and more
• Audio: AAC, Opus, FLAC, ALAC, MP3, Vorbis, plus full spatial-audio support — Dolby Atmos, TrueHD, MPEG-H 3D, IAMF
• HDR-to-SDR tone mapping (Hable, Reinhard, Mobius, BT.2390, Clip)
• Full Dolby Vision pipeline (Profile 5, 7, 8.1, 8.4) with automatic fallback
• Subtitle conversion, passthrough, and burning across 14+ formats
• HLS and DASH adaptive streaming with variant-ladder builder
• Audio CD ripping with AccurateRip verification and whole-disc fidelity
• Watch folders, conditional rules, pre/post-encode actions, scheduled jobs
• VMAF and SSIM quality metrics, bitrate/frame-rate heatmaps, A/B preview
• Encoding profiles with import/export and 7+ built-in presets
• Cloud delivery to S3, GCS, Backblaze B2, Azure, Dropbox, OneDrive, Google Drive and more
• Metadata lookup across TMDB, TheTVDB, MusicBrainz, Discogs, FanArt.tv
• CLI tool (meedya-convert) for CI pipelines and render farms
• Full drag-and-drop, queue management, real-time progress, and pause/resume
• Native dark & light mode, HiDPI, VoiceOver, and full keyboard accessibility

Ideal for creators, editors, broadcasters, and studios who need reliable conversion without the complexity of command-line tools or the cost of professional suites.
